Meet the Founder

I am Vivek S, an IT professional serving in the Indian Army's defense sector. Born and raised in the breathtaking region of Kanyakumari, I have had the privilege of calling India's southernmost point my home my entire life.

My deep connection with this magical land has given me intimate knowledge of every beach, temple, and hidden gem that most tourists never discover. From secluded coves where you can watch the sunrise in solitude to local eateries serving the most authentic Tamil cuisine, I know every corner of this paradise.

As an IT professional with a passion for travel and exploration, I created Venad Tamilan to share my love for Kanyakumari with the world. My mission is to help travelers experience the authentic essence of this remarkable destination while promoting sustainable tourism that benefits our local communities.
